The Island Cravings Journey

Our Tropical Roots

Founded in 2018 in the heart of Trinidad, Island Cravings began with a simple mission: to bring the authentic flavors of the Caribbean to the modern table. What started as a small family recipe for Guava Tarts soon expanded into a beloved local brand.

In response to the changing landscape of 2026, we transitioned to a click-and-mortar model. This shift allows us to centralize our operations and focus on delivering artisanal treats directly to your doorstep.
